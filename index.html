Multi-Agent ORB-SLAM

Two are better than one, because they have a good return for their labor: if either of them falls down, one can help the other up.

Two robots exploring can cover the same space in half the time.

If the two robots can identify each other, they can use recognition of the other robot as an additional opportunity for loop closure.

Requires combining map data from ORB-SLAM running on each robot

Datasets

Initial testing was done on the KITTI dataset

A custom dataset with tagged "robots" was also created from the second floor of the EECS building at the University of Michigan
